Quick Reference for ImportGenius Data Removal

ImportGenius Contact:
- Website: importgenius.com/contact
- Email: support@importgenius.com
- Subject: "Privacy Request - Data Removal"

Required Information:
- Company name (exact match to listings)
- Alternative names/DBAs
- Specific records to remove
- Business justification
- Contact authorization

Process:
1. Email removal request with required details
2. Reference privacy policy section on data removal
3. Request confirmation of processing
4. Follow up if no response within 10 days

Expected Timeline: 3-6 weeks

Notes:
- ImportGenius has specific format requirements
- May require additional business verification
- Some historical data may be retained
- New imports may reappear without ongoing privacy measures

For comprehensive removal and monitoring, consider Remova.org services.
