# ImportGenius Removal Guide
## Zoominfo Trade Data Division

### Platform Overview
ImportGenius is Zoominfo's trade intelligence platform that provides:
- US import/export records
- Supplier and customer identification
- Competitive intelligence reports
- Supply chain mapping
- Market analysis tools

### Data Sources
ImportGenius aggregates data from:
- US Customs and Border Protection (CBP)
- Bills of lading
- Shipping manifests
- Port authorities
- Partner databases

### Business Impact of Exposure
**Competitive Risks**:
- Competitors can identify your suppliers and poach them
- Your pricing strategies become visible through shipment values
- Market timing advantages disappear when competitors track your activities
- New product launches get spoiled by leaked supplier information

### Removal Procedure

#### Step 1: Data Discovery
1. Visit **importgenius.com**
2. Search for all company name variations:
   - Full legal name
   - DBA names
   - Abbreviations
   - Subsidiary names
3. Document all findings with screenshots
4. Note specific data types (shipments, suppliers, customers)

#### Step 2: Prepare Documentation
**Gather Required Information**:
- Legal business name and EIN
- All trade names and DBAs
- Business registration documentation
- List of specific data to be removed
- Screenshots of current exposure

#### Step 3: Submit Privacy Request
**Contact Method**: Online privacy form or email
**Website**: importgenius.com/privacy
**Email**: privacy@zoominfo.com

**Request Template**:
```
Subject: CCPA/GDPR Data Removal Request - [Company Name]

Dear ImportGenius Privacy Team,

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and applicable data protection regulations, I request immediate removal of all data related to [Company Name] from your platform.

Company Information:
- Legal Name: [Full Company Name]
- EIN: [Tax ID if applicable]
- Trade Names: [List all DBAs]
- Business Type: [Corporation/LLC/etc.]

Data Removal Request:
Please remove ALL data including:
- Import/export records
- Supplier relationships
- Customer information  
- Shipment details
- Company profiles
- Analytics and reports

Legal Basis:
This request is made under:
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) if applicable
- Right to deletion/erasure

Please confirm receipt within 5 business days and complete removal within 30 days. Provide evidence that data has been permanently deleted.

Regards,
[Name]
[Title]
[Company]
[Contact Information]
```

#### Step 4: Follow-Up Protocol
**Day 5**: Email confirmation of receipt
**Day 15**: Status update request  
**Day 30**: Verify completion and request proof
**Day 45**: Escalate if not resolved

#### Step 5: Verification Process
1. Search platform again for your company
2. Verify all profiles are removed
3. Check that supplier/customer data is cleaned
4. Test various search terms and name variations
5. Screenshot clean results for records

### Escalation Procedures
If ImportGenius doesn't comply:

**Level 1**: Zoominfo Corporate Privacy
- Email: privacy@zoominfo.com
- Phone: 1-866-904-9666

**Level 2**: Legal Department
- Email: legal@zoominfo.com
- Reference: Data Subject Rights Request

**Level 3**: Regulatory Complaints
- California Attorney General (CCPA violations)
- State data protection authorities

### Platform-Specific Considerations

**ImportGenius Features to Address**:
- **Company profiles**: Full removal required
- **Shipment tracking**: Historical data deletion
- **Supplier networks**: Relationship mapping removal
- **Analytics dashboards**: Exclusion from all reports
- **API access**: Ensure data not available via API

### Timeline Expectations
- **Initial response**: 5-10 business days
- **Data processing**: 15-30 business days  
- **Full removal**: 30-45 business days
- **Verification**: Additional 7-14 days

### Success Metrics
✅ Company search returns no results
✅ Supplier/customer relationships are hidden
✅ Historical import/export records removed
✅ Company excluded from all analytics
✅ API queries return no data

### Ongoing Maintenance
**Monthly Monitoring**:
- Search for company reappearance
- Check new data hasn't been added
- Monitor for policy changes

**Quarterly Review**:
- Comprehensive platform audit
- Update removal requests if needed
- Document compliance status

### Important Legal Notes
- **Data retention**: ImportGenius may retain some data for legal compliance
- **Third-party sharing**: Request disclosure of data sharing partners
- **Reindexing**: New data may appear if business continues importing/exporting
- **Backup systems**: Confirm deletion from all backup and archival systems

### Documentation Requirements
Keep detailed records of:
- All correspondence with ImportGenius
- Screenshots of data before and after removal
- Confirmation emails and removal certificates
- Timeline of all interactions
- Any compliance issues or delays
