Panjiva Removal Guide


S&P Global Trade Intelligence Platform

Overview


Panjiva is the world's largest trade intelligence platform, owned by S&P Global. It contains detailed shipping records, supplier relationships, and trade data from over 190 countries. Companies use Panjiva to:
- Find new suppliers and customers
- Track competitor activities
- Analyze market trends
- Conduct due diligence

Why Remove Your Data


Competitive Intelligence Risk: Your supplier relationships, shipping volumes, and business partnerships are visible to competitors who can:
- Poach your key suppliers
- Undercut your pricing
- Copy your supply chain strategies
- Time their market entries to compete with you

Removal Process

#### Step 1: Identify Your Company Data

1

Go to panjiva.com


2

Search for your company name and variations


3

Note all profiles and data entries


4

Screenshot everything for evidence

#### Step 2: Prepare Your Request
Required Information:
- Full legal company name
- All DBA names and variations
- Business registration number
- Contact email for correspondence
- Specific data you want removed

#### Step 3: Submit Removal Request
Email Address: privacy@panjiva.com
Subject: "Data Removal Request - [Your Company Name]"

Template Email:
```
Dear Panjiva Privacy Team,

I am writing to request the removal of all data related to [Company Name] from your platform under applicable privacy regulations.

Company Details:
- Legal Name: [Full Legal Name]
- DBA Names: [List all variations]
- Registration: [Business registration number]
- Contact: [Your email]

Please remove all:
- Company profiles
- Shipping records
- Supplier/customer relationships
- Trade data and analytics

I have attached screenshots showing the current data exposure.

Please confirm removal within 30 days and provide evidence that data has been deleted.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Title]
[Company]
```

#### Step 4: Follow Up
- 7 days: Check if they acknowledged your request
- 14 days: Follow up if no response
- 30 days: Escalate to S&P Global privacy team if not resolved

#### Step 5: Verify Removal

1

Search for your company again


2

Check that all data is removed


3

Document the clean results

Escalation Contacts


If Panjiva doesn't respond:
- S&P Global Privacy Office: privacy@spglobal.com
- Data Protection Officer: dpo@spglobal.com

Expected Timeline


- Response: 5-10 business days
- Removal: 15-30 business days
- Full deletion: 30-45 business days

Important Notes


- Not retroactive: Only stops new data collection
- Re-indexing risk: May need periodic monitoring
- Business impact: Consider which data relationships are critical before removal
- Documentation: Keep all correspondence for compliance records
