Quick Reference for Panjiva Data Removal

Panjiva Contact:
- Website: panjiva.com/contact
- Email: privacy@panjiva.com
- Subject: "Data Removal Request - [Company Name]"

Required Information:
- Full legal entity name
- All known variants/DBAs
- EIN/DUNS (if available)
- Specific data to be removed
- Authorized contact information

Process:
1. Submit removal request via contact form
2. Include business justification
3. Follow up within 5-7 business days
4. Monitor for compliance

Expected Timeline: 2-4 weeks

Notes:
- Panjiva may require additional verification
- Some data may persist in cached results
- Consider ongoing monitoring

For professional assistance, contact Remova.org Opt-Out Desk service.
